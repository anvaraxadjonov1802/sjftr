Nuqtadan tekislikkacha bo‘lgan masofa

1.         

 Qirrasi 1 sm ga teng ABCDA1B1C1D1 kubda  A nuqtadan  CB1D1 tekislikkacha bo‘lgan  masofani toping.

 

2.         

 Qirrasi 1 sm ga teng ABCDA1B1C1D1 kubda  A nuqtadan  BDC1 tekislikkacha bo‘lgan  masofani toping.

 

3.         

Uchburchakli  muntazam ABCA1B1C 1prizmada barcha qirralari 1 sm dan, A nuqtadan  BCA1 tekislikkacha bo‘lgan  masofani toping.

 

 

 

4.         

Uchburchakli  muntazam ABCA1B1C1 prizmada barcha qirralari 1 sm dan, A nuqtadan  SA1B1 tekislikkacha bo‘lgan  masofani toping.

 

 

5.         
To‘rtburchakli muntazam SABCD  piramidada barcha qirralari 1 sm dan, A nuqtadan  SCD tekislikkacha bo‘lgan  masofani toping.

 

 

 

6.          Oltiburchakli muntazam SABCDEF piramidaning asosining tomoni 1 sm, yon qirrasi 2 sm  bo‘lsa, A nuqtadan  SDE tekislikkacha bo‘lgan  masofani toping.


7.         

Oltiburchakli muntazam ABCDEFA1B1C1D1E1F1prizmada barcha qirralari 1 sm dan, A nuqtadan  DEA1 tekislikkacha bo‘lgan  masofani toping.

 

 

 

8.         

Oltiburchakli muntazam ABCDEFA1B1C1D1E1F1prizmada barcha qirralari 1 sm dan, A nuqtadan  DEF1 tekislikkacha bo‘lgan  masofani toping.
